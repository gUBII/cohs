File: scheduling-unset.php

Purpose
- Clears timeclock or project cookies and redirects back to clock in/out UI.

Primary flow
- If unset=timeclock, clears timeclockstat.
- Else clears projectid.
- Redirects to clock_in-out.php via auto-submit.

Inputs
- GET: unset.
- Cookies: userid, timeclockstat, projectid.

Outputs
- Auto-submit redirect HTML.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Removes cookies.

Risks / Notes
- No auth checks; GET-driven cookie reset.

Related
- scheduling-set.php
