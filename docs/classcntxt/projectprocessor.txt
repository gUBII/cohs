File: projectprocessor.php

Purpose
- Service agreement workflows, signatures, project/profile updates, and client communication book entries.

Primary flow
- Updates project and client agreement fields in multiple steps.
- Handles signature uploads (base64) and agreement images.
- Writes communication book/project form entries with attachments.
- Creates projects and campaign/lead forms (later in file).

Inputs
- POST: processor with agreement data, project ids, client ids, signatures, images, category form fields.
- Cookies: userid, dbname, projectidx, projectidsc.

Outputs
- JS alerts and redirects.

Data/DB
- Tables: project, uerp_user, project_forms, project_sc_forms, project_multi_image, project_sc_multi_image, audit.

Side effects
- Updates project/client data and stores signatures/images under media/.

Risks / Notes
- Raw SQL and large file uploads; relies on cookies for context.
