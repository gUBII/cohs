File: cookie_manager.php

Purpose
- Debug utility to display current cookies and then clear them.

Primary flow
- Iterates cookies and echoes key/value pairs.
- Iterates cookies again and expires them.

Inputs
- Cookies: all.

Outputs
- Echoed list of cookies.
- All cookies cleared.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Clears all cookies for the current path.

Risks / Notes
- Exposes cookie values in plain output; avoid in production.

Related
- logout.php, login.php
