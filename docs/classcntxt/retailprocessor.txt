File: retailprocessor.php

Purpose
- Retail/inventory actions: products, purchase vouchers, and generic table updates.

Primary flow
- GlobalSuspend/StatusSuspend for arbitrary tables.
- Inserts/updates products with images and policy fields.
- Inserts purchase vouchers and processes purchases into inventory queue.
- Includes ledger_class updates (continued in file).

Inputs
- GET: processor=StatusSuspend/GlobalSuspend with table/field ids.
- POST: processor new/edit product, purchase voucher actions, delete/update records.
- Cookies: dbname.

Outputs
- JS alerts and redirects.

Data/DB
- Tables: product, purchase_voucher, product_in_queue, ledger_class, etc.

Side effects
- Uploads product images to media/products.

Risks / Notes
- Dynamic table updates and raw SQL inputs.
