File: admin_email_tester.php

Purpose
- Admin/test endpoint to send a recovery email via PHPMailer.

Primary flow
- Configures PHPMailer with Gmail SMTP and app password.
- Sends a hardcoded recovery email to a fixed recipient.
- Alerts success or error.

Inputs
- None (hardcoded recipient and content).

Outputs
- JS alert.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Sends email.

Risks / Notes
- Contains Gmail credentials and fixed recipient.
- Should not be exposed in production.
