File: email_task.php

Purpose
- Sends task assignment email to a staff member via PHPMailer.

Primary flow
- On POST processor=tasks_manager:
  - Reads semail and employeename from POST.
  - Sends SMTP email via mail.goodwillcare.net.
  - Redirects back to index.php?smsbd=tasks-manager.

Inputs
- POST: processor=tasks_manager, semail, employeename, clientname.

Outputs
- Email sent message; redirects via auto-submit.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Sends outbound email.

Risks / Notes
- Hardcoded SMTP credentials.
- SMTP debug enabled.

Related
- email_sender.php, email_roster*.php
