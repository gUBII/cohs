File: dataprocessor_1.php

Purpose
- Admin data maintenance for warehouses, NDIS catalog items, ledger structures, vouchers, and product metadata.

Primary flow
- GET processor: status suspend and global suspend (dynamic table/column updates).
- POST processor: create/update warehouse, NDIS items, ledger classes/groups/accounts, vouchers, product attributes/brands/categories, designations.
- Writes audit logs for many inserts/updates.

Inputs
- GET: processor, tid, pid, status, sid, sid1.
- POST: processor with many entity-specific fields; file uploads for images.
- Cookies: dbname, userid.

Outputs
- JS alerts; no JSON.

Data/DB
- Tables: warehouse, ndis_support_catelogue2, ndis_line_numbers (root DB), ledger_class/group/accounts_ledger/sub_ledger, receipt_voucher, payment_voucher, product_attributes/brands/category, designation, audit.

Side effects
- Creates/updates master data and uploads images to media/*.

Risks / Notes
- Dynamic table/column names and raw inputs in SQL.
- No explicit auth/CSRF checks.
