File: email-auth.php

Purpose
- Sends account recovery OTP and sets cookies for reset flow.

Primary flow
- Reads receiver email/name/dbn/rid from GET.
- Generates random OTP, stores md5 hash in cookie vcode.
- Stores email, rid, dbn in cookies.
- Sends plaintext email via mail() with OTP.
- Redirects to resetpassword.php on success.

Inputs
- GET: reml, rnm, dbn, rid.

Outputs
- Sends email, redirects to resetpassword.php.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Sets cookies: vcode, emailid, rid, dbn.

Risks / Notes
- No auth/validation on GET params; OTP/email can be triggered by anyone.
- Uses md5 for OTP hash.

Related
- security.php, resetpassword.php
