File: clockinout2.php

Purpose
- Compact clock-out widget for active timeclock session.

Primary flow
- Uses timeclockin cookie to compute elapsed time.
- Renders a clock-out form that posts to scheduling-geo-out.php.
- Auto-submits clock-out if day changes.

Inputs
- Cookies: timeclockin, shiftname, shiftclockin, shiftclockout.

Outputs
- HTML widget with elapsed timer.

Data/DB
- Tables: theme (color settings).

Side effects
- None beyond posting to scheduling-geo-out.php.

Risks / Notes
- Relies on cookies and client-side timing.

Related
- scheduling-geo-out.php
