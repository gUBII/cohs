File: forgot.php

Purpose
- Account recovery request UI (email + phone).

Primary flow
- Clears all cookies.
- Renders form to collect email and registered phone.
- Posts to security.php for recovery handling (sends recovery link/OTP).
- Includes head-light.php and scripts-light.php.

Inputs
- GET: sourcefrom (optional styling).
- POST: femail, fphone (sent to security.php).

Outputs
- HTML form for recovery request.

Data/DB
- None directly.

Side effects
- Clears cookies client-side and server-side.

Risks / Notes
- No CSRF protection.

Related
- security.php (recovery handler)
- resetpassword.php
