File: email_roster.php

Purpose
- Sends shift allocation email to a staff member via PHPMailer.

Primary flow
- On POST processor=shiftallocation:
  - Reads semail and employeename from POST.
  - Sends SMTP email via mail.goodwillcare.net.

Inputs
- POST: processor=shiftallocation, semail, employeename, clientname.

Outputs
- Email sent message or error.

Data/DB
- None.

Side effects
- Sends outbound email.

Risks / Notes
- Hardcoded SMTP credentials.
- SMTP debug enabled.

Related
- email_roster_suspend.php, email_roster_update.php
