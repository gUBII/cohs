File: appointmentprocessor.php

Purpose
- CRUD for appointments_manager with attachments and audit logging.

Primary flow
- POST appointmentlist: inserts appointment and attachments.
- POST editappointment: updates appointment and attachments.
- GET StatusSuspend: toggles record status on a table.

Inputs
- POST: processor, employeeid, clientid, title, detail, date1/date2, priority, mode, projectid, images.
- GET: processor=StatusSuspend, tid, pid, status.
- Cookies: userid, dbname.

Outputs
- JS alerts and redirects.

Data/DB
- Tables: appointments_manager, project_multi_image, audit.

Side effects
- Creates/updates appointments; uploads images to media/appointments.

Risks / Notes
- Raw SQL from request inputs.
