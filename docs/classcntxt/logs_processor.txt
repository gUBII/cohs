File: logs_processor.php

Purpose
- Lead/CRM helper for stage updates and cookie-based filters.

Primary flow
- Updates lead stage and follow-up date.
- Sets cookies for lead/client/activity filters and redirects.

Inputs
- POST: processor, stageid, stageupdater, datemanager, b_lead, cid, leadsearch, activity_id, url, id.

Outputs
- JS alerts and redirects.

Data/DB
- Table: leads.

Side effects
- Updates lead fields and sets cookies.

Risks / Notes
- No explicit auth; raw SQL from request inputs.
