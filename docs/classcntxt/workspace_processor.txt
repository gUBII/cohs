File: workspace_processor.php

Purpose
- Workspace/project setup, budgeting, and service agreement processing.

Primary flow
- Sets budget project cookie.
- Updates quotes and status.
- Writes categorized budgeting into project and project_budget tables.
- Updates project details, managed_by, schedules, agreement data, signatures, and service fees.
- Generates service agreement addons and shift templates (continued in file).

Inputs
- POST: processor with project, budget, agreement, and signature fields.
- GET: processor StatusSuspend/GlobalSuspend.
- Cookies: userid, dbname, ws_invoice.

Outputs
- JS alerts and redirects.

Data/DB
- Tables: quotes, project, project_budget, project_schedules, ndis_invoices, ndis_invoices_addon, service_agreement_addon, leave_holidays, audit.

Side effects
- Updates project budget data and creates service agreement addon lines.

Risks / Notes
- Raw SQL from request inputs; relies on cookies for context.

Related
- workspace_budget_processor.php, projectprocessor.php
