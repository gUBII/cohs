File: login.php

Purpose
- Public login page for /cohs. Clears cookies, shows login UI, and posts to security.php via iframe.

Primary flow
- Clears all cookies (loop over $_COOKIE and expire).
- Includes include.php and head-light.php.
- Renders login form that posts uid/upass to security.php (target iframe processor).
- Adds animated branding and a hidden iframe pointing to blank-login.php for messages.
- Includes scripts-light.php.
- JS deleteAllCookies() runs onload to clear cookies client-side.

Inputs
- Query string: sourcefrom (used for optional CSS block).
- Cookies: all are cleared.

Outputs
- HTML login page and POST to security.php.

Data/DB
- include.php may connect to master DB if PHP_SELF is /cohs/login.php.

Side effects
- Expunges all cookies server-side and client-side.

Risks / Notes
- Cookie clearing is broad; may affect other apps on same domain.

Related
- security.php (auth processor)
- blank-login.php (iframe messages)
- head-light.php, scripts-light.php
