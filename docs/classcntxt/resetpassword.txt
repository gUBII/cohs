File: resetpassword.php

Purpose
- UI for entering OTP and new password to complete account recovery.

Primary flow
- Renders form asking for OTP, new password, confirm password.
- Posts to security.php for processing.
- Includes head-light.php and scripts-light.php.
- Clears cookies on load via deleteAllCookies().

Inputs
- GET: sourcefrom (used for optional styling).
- POST: fotp, npass, cpass (sent to security.php).

Outputs
- HTML form for reset.

Data/DB
- None directly; security.php performs DB updates.

Side effects
- Clears all cookies client-side.

Risks / Notes
- Uses plain POST without CSRF.

Related
- security.php (recovery confirmation)
- forgot.php (recovery request)
